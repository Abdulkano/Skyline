# Skyline TV 🌆📺

**Nigeria's Immersive Streaming Platform**

Official repository for Skyline TV - A next-generation streaming platform by a leading Nigerian television station.

**Website**: [www.skylinetv.ng](https://www.skylinetv.ng)

## Overview

Skyline TV is a cutting-edge streaming platform that brings premium Nigerian and African content to viewers worldwide. We're revolutionizing how audiences consume television by combining traditional broadcast quality with modern streaming technology.

Our mission is to deliver immersive, high-quality entertainment while celebrating African storytelling on a global stage.

## Features

- 📺 **Live TV & On-Demand Streaming** - Watch live broadcasts and catch up on your favorite shows anytime
- 🎬 **Premium Content Library** - Curated collection of Nigerian, African, and international content
- 🌍 **Global Accessibility** - Stream from anywhere in the world with optimized performance
- 📱 **Multi-Device Support** - Seamless viewing across phones, tablets, and smart TVs
- 👥 **Personalized Profiles** - Customized recommendations and watchlists for each viewer
- ⚡ **High-Quality Streaming** - Adaptive bitrate technology for smooth playback on any connection
- 🎤 **Live Events** - Stream live entertainment, music, and special broadcasts
